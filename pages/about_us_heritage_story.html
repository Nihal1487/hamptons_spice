Our Journey Through Time

From humble beginnings in local markets to becoming a trusted global partner - every milestone shaped our commitment to excellence.

2009 - The Beginning

Hamptons Spice was founded with a vision to bridge traditional Indian spice farming with modern export standards. Started with partnerships with 5 farming families in Kerala and Karnataka.

2012 - Quality Milestone

Achieved our first FSSAI certification and established quality control protocols. Expanded to 15 farming partnerships and launched our first international export to the UK market.

2016 - Organic Excellence

Obtained USDA Organic certification and established our organic farming program. Partnered with 50+ organic farmers and expanded to serve North American and European markets.

2020 - Global Reach

Achieved ISO 22000 certification and expanded to serve 25+ countries. Established regional distribution centers and launched our digital client portal for seamless order management.

2025 - Today

Leading premium spice exporter with 500+ global partners, 200+ farming families, and a commitment to sustainable growth. Continuing to bridge tradition with innovation for the future of spice trading.

Celebrating India's Spice Legacy

For over 4,000 years, India has been the world's spice garden. We honor this rich heritage while building bridges to global markets.

Ancient Trade Routes to Modern Markets

The Spice Route Legacy

From the ancient Silk Road to modern shipping lanes, Indian spices have traveled the world, carrying stories of culture, tradition, and unmatched quality that we continue today.

Traditional Processing Wisdom

Generations of farmers have perfected sun-drying, hand-sorting, and natural processing methods that preserve the essential oils and authentic flavors that make Indian spices legendary.

Regional Specialties

Each region of India contributes unique spices - Kerala's black pepper, Kashmir's saffron, Tamil Nadu's turmeric - each with distinct characteristics shaped by local climate and soil.
